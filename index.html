Convenient Location

The Location is ideal for business or leisure stay and our convenient location offers easy access to the entire city. Paddington Rail Station and the Heathrow Express service are a 10-minute taxi ride from Marylebone Inn. Buckingham Palace is within 2 miles from the building. In the heart of the West End, Marylebone Inn is surrounded by restaurants and shops, which can be reached in 5 minutes on foot. Regent’s Park is also a 5-minute walk away and the city’s popular theatres are just Tube ride away.
